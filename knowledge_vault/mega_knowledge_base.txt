
=== DOMAIN: ACCESSIBLE HEALTHCARE ===
H1: Healthcare Rights
Fact: Every patient has a legal right to receive medical information in a format they can understand. This includes Braille, large print, or digital screen-reader-ready files.
Fact: Hospitals must provide qualified sign language interpreters for deaf patients at no extra cost.
Fact: Medical devices like blood pressure monitors and insulin pumps now have 'Voice Guidance' models to help blind patients manage their health independently.

=== DOMAIN: INCLUSIVE FINANCE ===
F1: Banking Empowerment
Fact: Most modern banking apps include 'High Contrast' and 'Voice Guidance' features to help users with low vision manage their money securely.
Fact: Financial institutions are required to have 'Simple Terms' versions of loan documents to help users with cognitive disabilities understand their commitments.
Fact: Biometric access (fingerprint or face scan) is a crucial accessibility feature for those who struggle to remember complex passwords or have motor control challenges.

=== DOMAIN: EMERGENCY & SAFETY ===
E1: SOS Protocols
Fact: The '911' or '112' systems in many regions now support 'Text-to-911' for users who are deaf, hard of hearing, or have speech disabilities.
Fact: In emergencies, users can activate 'Emergency SOS' on most smartphones by rapidly pressing the power button five times.
Fact: Specialized 'Safety Check' features can automatically alert family members if a user with mobility issues doesn't arrive at a destination on time.

=== DOMAIN: LEGAL ACCESSIBILITY LAWS ===
L1: The Global Standards
Fact: The RPWD Act of 2016 (India) mandates that all public buildings and transport systems must be accessible to people with disabilities.
Fact: The ADA (Americans with Disabilities Act) protects against discrimination in all areas of public life, including jobs, schools, and transportation.
Fact: The European Accessibility Act (EAA) ensures that products like ATMs, banking services, and e-books are accessible across the EU.

=== DOMAIN: EVERYDAY TECH SUPPORT ===
T1: Simplified Navigation
Fact: Windows 11 'Focus Sessions' help users with ADHD reduce distractions and manage their time more effectively.
Fact: Common Keyboard Shortcut: Press 'Windows + Plus sign' to open the Magnifier tool instantly.
Fact: Android 'Live Transcribe' provides real-time captions for the sounds and speech happening around you.
